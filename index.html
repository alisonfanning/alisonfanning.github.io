About Me

I'm a UX Designer based in Boston, MA. I have experience working in ed-tech and nonprofits. Whether I'm working with another department or a customer, I enjoy uncovering the business need or objective behind a request and designing from there. My focus is on creating intuitive workflows that give users the best experience possible.
